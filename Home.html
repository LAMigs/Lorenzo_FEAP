EYES ON A.I. is an entertainment company that functions as a record label, talent agency, and A.I. development company. It introduces a fresh concept to the Philippines that puts both pop idol culture and technology together. As the Hallyu Wave greatly influences our Filipino youth today, EYES ON A.I. gives the chance for them to show potential and eventually enter the idol life. Knowing that the Philippines is fond of any other culture aside from their own, we also aim to erase the mentality of our fellow Filipinos that it’s always better if it’s from another country. More specifically the type of thinking that Filipino music is automatically equal to “jeje”. Aside from that, not only does EYES ON A.I. produce artists, but also Artificial Intelligence (A.I.) counterparts of them. Its main goal is to be the “gateway” of the future’s entertainment industry, and as well as to promote performing arts and Filipino music in the Philippines.
